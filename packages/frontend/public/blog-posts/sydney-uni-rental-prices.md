## What are the average rents by unit type near Sydney Uni?
- 2B1B units average AUD 815/week, increasing to 846/week if you want ≤30 min commute.
- 2B2B units are much higher at AUD 1100+ per week, with closer locations rising above 1165/week.
- 3B2B units average around AUD 1450+/week, going above 1500/week for central locations.
- 1B/studio apartments average AUD 815–866/week, depending on commute time.

These numbers show a clear trend: the closer to the university, the higher the weekly rent, especially for larger apartments.
