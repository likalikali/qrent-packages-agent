The surrounding environment in Australia has a direct impact on safety and convenience. Students should consider access control, cleanliness, nearby supermarkets (Coles, Aldi, Woolworths), pharmacies, convenience stores, gyms, restaurants, and transport links. Real commuting times (including waiting) to school or the city should be calculated carefully.
